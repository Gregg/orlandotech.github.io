Meetup

The Orlando Tech Meetup is a monthly event organized through meetup.com that gives local technology entreprenseurs a platform to demo their product to a live audience and get real-time feedback. The Orlando Tech Meetup meets on the 3rd Thursday of every month with 5 companies demoing for 4 minutes each and the audience gets 4 minutes to ask questions after each demo!
